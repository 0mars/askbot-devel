osqa by rickross

An open source Q&A(question and answer) eco-system

OSQA, the open source Q&A system, is a community knowledge sharing application derived from Mike Chen's outstanding CNPROG and inspired by popular, proprietary systems like Yahoo Answers, StackOverflow and others.
